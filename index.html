Provision Law Firm

Located in Burnsville but serving clients throughout the Twin Cities metro, Provision Law Firm PLLC is an estates and trusts law firm that focuses exclusively on the estates of individuals and families planning for their futures, as well as handling the estates of loved ones who have passed. All of us should have certain basic elements of an estate plan to protect ourselves in the case of incapacity and to protect our loved ones in the case of death. At Provision Law Firm, Attorney Carrie A. Renner will listen to your unique circumstances and help you evaluate your estate planning options so that you can choose a plan that is best for you.
